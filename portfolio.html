Project Portfolio

The Project Portfolio is intended to become a centralized place where computing instructors can find, compare, adapt, and share FORAP-based projects. We already have a collection of packaged projects; the next step is turning that collection into a public platform for discovery and contribution.

Projects in the portfolio

The portfolio brings together a diverse set of reusable projects across multiple computing areas, codebase contexts, difficulty levels, and durations.
